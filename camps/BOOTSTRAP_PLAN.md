## CONCIERGE CALL SCRIPT

For the calls you sell:

**Before call (5 min prep):**
- Look at their kids' ages
- Note any camps they've already viewed/saved

**Call structure (30 min):**

1. **Intro (2 min)**
   "Thanks for booking! Tell me about your kids and what you're hoping for this summer."

2. **Gather requirements (5 min)**
   - Ages of kids
   - Weeks needing coverage
   - Budget per week
   - Interests (STEM, art, sports, nature)
   - Location preferences
   - Schedule constraints (drop-off/pickup times)

3. **Show options (10 min)**
   - Share screen, use PDX Camps
   - Filter by their criteria
   - Show 3-4 options per week
   - Explain tradeoffs

4. **Build the plan (10 min)**
   - Add camps to their planner
   - Fill each week
   - Note backup options

5. **Wrap up (3 min)**
   - Review the plan
   - Highlight registration deadlines
   - "I'll send a follow-up email with everything"

**After call:**
- Email summary with plan link
- Registration deadlines listed
- Ask for testimonial/referral
